Vodacom  M-pawa
the brief

Vodacom Mpawa is a mobile wallet that allows Vodacom users to save money from their mobile phone, with Commercial Bank of Africa (CBA) as the financial partner  running a campaign to grow the average deposit balance per user through a campaign dubbed JIONGEZE NA MPAWA, the campaign rewarded 200 winners daily winning double their closing balance on MPawa, One Daily Winner winning 1 million, One weekly winner won 20 million and Grand prize of 100 million for a period of 45 days.

the solution

Smart Codes was tasked with the assignment of developing the promo tool to perform the draws to select the various winners, functionality included integration
with the CBA core banking system, selecting daily transaction logs for Mpawa, filtering for withdrawals, creating a database within the tool and profile, awarding customers points based on MPawa activity, blacklisting ineligible customers. Running draws in the presence of Gaming board officials and automatically generating reports and crediting customers accounts.
